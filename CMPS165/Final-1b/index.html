Voter Registration and Turnout for November 2016 Election

Purpose: Firstly, visualize the voter registration percentage by state, age, and other metrics.
Secondly, visualize the actual voter turnout for 2016 election by the same metrics as the registration.
Finally, also add how the final state vote went, and perhaps the electoral votes for each state.
